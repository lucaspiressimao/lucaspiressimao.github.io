ABOUT ME

LUCAS PIRES SIMÃO

Hello, I am Lucas Pires Simão, a DevOps engineer passionate about innovation and technology. With over 10 years of experience, I have specialized in developing efficient and scalable solutions, facing challenges in companies of various sizes and structures. My career is marked by the implementation of infrastructures and tools, mainly AWS, and the promotion of the DevOps culture. I value collaboration and the constant pursuit of improvement, whether in automation with Terraform or in implementing practices that increase the frequency of deployment.

Beyond my technical expertise, I dedicate myself to continuous learning and the development of interpersonal and leadership skills. I follow market innovations and am always looking for new ways to contribute to the DevOps community, to whom I owe much of my learning. Outside of the professional environment, my hobbies are also oriented towards technology and innovation but within the maker world. I thank everyone who takes an interest in my professional journey and am open to new connections and opportunities to jointly drive innovation in DevOps. Check out my profile.
